About Us:

Fine Arc team has expertise and creative for design to residential and commercial projects. Our designers create both modern and traditional designs with a unique twist, turning any space into a powerful expression of the client's personality. Having a strong in-house technical foundation and with the support of a strong network of trades-people, fabricators, suppliers, manufacturers and vendors who share our vision, we continuously aim to capitalize into the emerging market requirement of creating professional environments for organizations that inspire, empower and revolutionize the lives, workplaces and recreations of the people inside.

The company goals and objectives mainly include:

    - Accurate delivery of services to our customer's requirement.
    - Offer the highest level of interior design fit out project standards and practices in the industry.
    -100% customer satisfaction.

We strive to accomplish a major market share by maintaining a mutual trust between ourselves and our customers. We have contributed significant value, not only to our clients but also to our employees and our community, through continual innovation and sustainable development.

Working mainly in neutral tones with colourful accents we use combinations of textures to create an extravagantly simple atmosphere. We focus on the physical effect of being in a room, incorporating tactile materials which exude quality through the way they look as well as the way they feel. Our spaces are uncluttered without feeling sparse, fusing international and local styles. We like to use a combination of locally made and imported products.
