Pushpa Bhat

(Founder, Fine Arc)

Pushpa Bhat, Founder and Design Principal of Fine Arc, is regarded as a leading authority on home fashion and interior design . She graduated from K.L.E College and began working in the design field in 2009. She was submerged into an extremely creative environment which flourished her love for architecture & interior design. She also enjoys exploring new ventures and traveling to different places to study intricacies of design. She has abundant knowledge of the aesthetics and the combinations when it comes to interiors, be it commercial or residential. Her passion and dream all started with one common point of making a great product with all the gained experiences and bringing a look of its own. She has worked with all the renowned firms as a Designer and execution specialist. The way she understands and interprets the emotions of the clients to reality is commendable. She is the one who believes in turning every pebble to pearl.

Shenoy Ranjal

(Co-founder)

Shenoy Ranjal has an overall experience of nine years in Sales and Operations. He is one of the key intellects behind the venture. With his skillset on operations and management he was able to contribute the most of it up to a level where we decided to launch Fine arc. With a creative background and a great sense of current trends with respect to the designs and architecture, the firm has turned into a portfolio worth looking and trusting for.
